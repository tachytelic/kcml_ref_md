Forms Designer - Working with Group boxes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

To add a group box to the current form click on the <img src="bitmaps/form0057.gif" data-align="BOTTOM" data-border="0" alt="form0057.gif" /> icon from the controls palette.

Group boxes are used to surround and provide a title to a group of controls. The caption text for a Group box is set with the

*Text\$* property. To include an accelerator key in the caption text simply insert an Ampersand before the required character.
