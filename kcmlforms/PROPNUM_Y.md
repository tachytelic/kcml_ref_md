Top (Generic property)

For a control this property specifies the distance from the top of the form to the top of the control.

In the case of the form itself it determines the distance from the top of the Windows desktop to the top of the form. It can also be used in conjunction with the [*Placement*](PROP_DLG_PLACEMENT.htm) property to change the physical position of the form.

The measurement is expressed in [Dialog Box Units](DialogBoxUnitsDLUs.htm).

.btnControl1.Top = 47
