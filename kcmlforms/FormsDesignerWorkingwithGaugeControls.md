Forms Designer - Working with Gauge Controls

------------------------------------------------------------------------

To add a gauge control to a form click on the <img src="bitmaps/form0056.gif" data-align="BOTTOM" data-border="0" alt="form0056.gif" />icon on the controls palette.

The gauge control control is used to display a progress bar to the user. It is most useful when a long time consuming task is being performed.
