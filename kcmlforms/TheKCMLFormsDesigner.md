The KCML Forms Designer

The KCML forms designer is a graphical design tool invoked by the KCML workbench to design forms before they are committed back into the KCML program. All of the information relating to a form is stored in the KCML program itself hidden inside the DEFFORM statement. When ever the form is edited, by right clicking on the DEFFORM and chosing Edit Form from the context menu, this information is passed transparently into the Forms Designer. The program is automatically updated with any changes within the forms designer when the forms designer is exited. Selecting the

*Cancel* option within the forms designer will cancel any edits made and will leave the program unchanged.
