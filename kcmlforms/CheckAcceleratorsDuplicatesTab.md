Check Accelerators - Duplicates Tab

This option is used to display any duplicate accelerator keys that exist in the current form. The duplicates can be removed by selecting the control and clicking on the Edit Caption option. Changing the position of the Ampersand (&) will change the accelerator key.

Click on the relevant section of the form to obtain further information.
