The Edit Menu - Delete option\
Shortcut - DEL\
Tool bar - <img src="bitmaps/the_0288.gif" data-align="BOTTOM" data-border="0" alt="bitmaps/the_0288.gif" />

This option is used to delete all of the currently selected controls. Controls are selected by left clicking on the form and dragging the selection box around the controls that are to be selected.
