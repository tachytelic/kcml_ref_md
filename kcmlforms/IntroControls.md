Controls

A KCML form is made up of any number of controls. Most controls are used to retrieve information from the user, for example a push button or an edit control while other controls simply provide information to the user like a static text control or a picture control.

KCML forms also support the 32 bit OCX standard allowing third party controls to be added to forms to dramatically extend their impact. Controls for such entities as 3D graphs, imaging, calendars etc., are available from numerous specialist suppliers.
