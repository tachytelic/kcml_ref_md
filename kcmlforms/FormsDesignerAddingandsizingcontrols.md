Forms Designer - Adding and sizing controls

To add new controls to the form, click on the required control on the controls palette then click on the form. The control will be placed at the current mouse pointer position. The control can be moved to a new position by dragging the control with the mouse.

To resize a control, click on the control and drag the controls sizing handles with the mouse.

As each control is added it is recommended that you set the anchor points for most controls, this will ensure that the controls remain in a tidy position if the form is resized.
