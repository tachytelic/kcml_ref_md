Changing the picture alignment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

To change the alignment of a picture in a KCML picture button the

*PictureAlignment* property is used. For example the following would change the picture position with the control to the bottom right hand corner of the control:

.picControl1.PictureAlignment = &.BottomRight
