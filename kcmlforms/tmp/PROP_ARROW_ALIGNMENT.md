**Specifies the alignment of the arrow**

The alignment style can be used to line up adjacent arrows. The example shows two arrow controls which have been aligned by KForm and given middle alignment styles.

<img src="/bitmaps/arrowalign.png" data-align="BOTTOM" data-border="0" width="250" height="200" alt="middle aligned arows" />

##### Example:


     .arrow.Alignment = &.Default
