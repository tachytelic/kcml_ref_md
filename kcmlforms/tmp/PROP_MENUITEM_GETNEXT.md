**Get next menu object**

Used to retrieve the next menu item on a menu. Normally used in conjunction with [MenuNext](PROP_MENUITEM_GETNEXT.htm) to enumerate through all the menu items on a menu.

##### Example:


     OBJECT a = .menuitem.MenuNext
