**Thickness of outline**

Specifies the thickness of the arrow's outline in dialog box units. The example shows an arrow with a heavy outline.

<img src="/bitmaps/arrowedgethick.png" data-align="BOTTOM" data-border="0" width="250" height="200" alt="thick arrow" />

##### Example:


     n = .arrow.EdgeThickness
