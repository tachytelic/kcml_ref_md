**Duplicate menu item as separator**

The Duplicate() method can only be used in the [Create()](PROP_FORM_CREATE.htm) event. This form of the menu item Duplicate() method will place a separator immediately after the menu item. For more information on duplicating menu items see [Duplicate(str, int)](PROP_MENU_DUPLICATE_CHILD.htm).

##### Example:


     .menuitem.Duplicate()
