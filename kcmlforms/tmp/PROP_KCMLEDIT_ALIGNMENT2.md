*(Left, Center, Right)*

**Specifies the alignment of text within the control**

Used to specify the alignment of the text within an Edit control. The available settings are ***Left,*** ***Center*** and ***Right***. It is a style property and cannot be set at runtime after the Create() event.

##### Example:


     .editControl.Alignment = &.Left
