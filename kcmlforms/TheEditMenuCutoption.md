The Edit Menu - Cut option\
Shortcut - Shift Delete\
Tool bar - <img src="bitmaps/the_0285.gif" data-align="BOTTOM" data-border="0" alt="bitmaps/the_0285.gif" />

Deletes the selected controls and places them into the Clip Board. The [Paste](TheEditMenuPasteOption.htm) option can be used to paste the controls back onto the form.
