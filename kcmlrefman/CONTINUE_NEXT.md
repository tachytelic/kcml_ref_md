CONTINUE NEXT <span style="font-size: 16pt ; ">command</span>

------------------------------------------------------------------------

General Form:\
\
     CONTINUE NEXT\
\

------------------------------------------------------------------------

The CONTINUE NEXT command will restart a program that has been stopped either with a [STOP](STOP.htm) or a [TRAP](TRAP.htm) statement or has been interrupted by a Ctrl+BREAK, and stop it again immediately before executing the [NEXT](FOR.htm) statement after the last iteration of the current [FOR ... NEXT](FOR.htm) loop.

The CONTINUE NEXT command can be executed directly within the Workbench either as a single keystroke or from the toolbar.
