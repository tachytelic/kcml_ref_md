LIST U

------------------------------------------------------------------------

General form:\
\
     LIST \[title\] U\
\
Where:\
\
          title           = alpha_variable or a literal string\
\

------------------------------------------------------------------------

The LIST U statement will list all of the available C user written routines and a summary of their expected arguments.

Example of output:

:LIST U\
R7_DATE2J                str TO int\
R7_J2DATE                int, int TO str
