LIST FROM'

------------------------------------------------------------------------

General Form:\
\
     \[@\]LIST \[title\] FROM ' subroutine-name\
\
Where:\
\
     title           = alpha_variable or a literal string\
\

------------------------------------------------------------------------

The LIST FROM' statement lists from the first line of the subroutine requested. If the optional @ sign precedes the reserved word LIST, then the listing will be taken from the currently selected global partition, if any.

The LIST FROM' operation can also be performed within the KCML Workbench by actioning the subroutine label.
