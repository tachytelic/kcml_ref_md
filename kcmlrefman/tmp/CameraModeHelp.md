Camera capture mode

| Constant name                       | Value | Description                 |
|-------------------------------------|-------|-----------------------------|
| \_CAMERACAPTURE_MODE_STILL          | 0x0   | Capture a still image       |
| \_CAMERACAPTURE_MODE_VIDEOONLY      | 0x1   | Capture video without audio |
| \_CAMERACAPTURE_MODE_VIDEOWITHAUDIO | 0x2   | Capture video and audio     |
